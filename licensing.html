Font Licensing

Font Licensing for Doc Comp, Mainframe, CCM and CRM systems

There has been a change in licensing laws since 2010 to a renewable model for  Document Composition, Mainframe, and CRM/CCM systems, both tenant level and hosted environments. It is a legal requirement that these licenses are implemented.

All of our application server licenses, known as EXT4, include provision for the support of AFP data streams, and digital transformation environments. These licenses are available for Windows System and non system fonts.

Terrapin are a global distributor for all major and most smaller font manufacturers. Licensing models differ between manufacturers, some offering a perpetual license, and some offer a renewable model.  The renewable model is based on a yearly renewable term. Multi year licenses are available, the maximum term being 5 years.

Please consult with us at your earliest opportunity with regard to available options.  We will always respond to your enquiry in a timely and professional manner.
